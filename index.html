Built for Emergencies

Everything you need in the moment.

From PIN protection to offline backup cards — Mberede is designed for real emergencies in real life.

🔐

PIN Protected

Your contacts are secured with a secret PIN. Only you and anyone you trust with the code can access them.

📞

Call & SMS

Call emergency contacts directly or send SMS from the app. Works with any phone that has Telegram.

📍

Location in SOS

SOS alerts include your GPS location so contacts know exactly where to find you.

☁️

VoIP Calls

Server-initiated calls connect you to your emergency contact even without a SIM in the device.

🔄

Multi-Account

Help others access their contacts from your device without logging out of your own account.

🖨️

Offline Backup Card

Generate a printable QR card with your contacts. Works even without internet on the finding device.

✅

Contact Consent

Contacts receive verification SMS so they consent to being part of your emergency network.

🌍

Pan-African

Built for Nigeria and the African continent. Supports local SMS providers and international formats.

📤

Data Export

Export or delete your data anytime. Fully GDPR/NDPR compliant with your privacy in mind.
